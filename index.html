Intro

Hello, my name is Gideon, and I am a dedicated Backend Developer with expertise in NestJS and Laravel PHP. My experience in backend development is complemented by a solid foundation in HTML, CSS (including Bootstrap, Less, and Tailwind), and JavaScript frameworks like Next.js and Vue. Additionally, I am proficient in using Laravel Livewire to create dynamic and responsive web applications.

Beyond coding, I have a strong background in Database Design, ensuring that the data layer of my projects is both robust and efficient. I am deeply committed to adhering to design patterns and best practices, which I believe are crucial for maintaining code quality and scalability. Proper documentation is another cornerstone of my development process, as it facilitates better collaboration and future maintenance.

System design is a particular area of interest for me, and I always strive to create architectures that are both scalable and resilient. Whether working independently or as part of a team, my goal is to deliver high-quality software solutions that meet and exceed expectations. You can check my work here.
